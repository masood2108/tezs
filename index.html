Medicine

LUMINA was founded in 2006 on a conviction: that specialist medicine should feel as humane as it is advanced. We built a practice where no patient ever waits more than 20 minutes, every consultation lasts as long as it needs to, and every opinion is evidence-based.

Our 24 specialists collaborate across disciplines, sharing patient notes in real-time so that your care is joined-up — not fragmented across siloed consultants.
